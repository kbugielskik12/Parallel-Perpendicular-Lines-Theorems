Parallel and Perpendicular Line Theorems

1. Perpendicular Lines Form Four Right Angles

When two lines are perpendicular, they intersect to make four right angles (90Â° each).

2. Two Perpendiculars to the Same Line are Parallel

If two different lines are each perpendicular to the same line, then those two lines are parallel to each other.

3. Perpendicular Transversal Theorem

If one line is perpendicular to one of two parallel lines, then it is also perpendicular to the other.

4. Parallel Transitive Theorem

If two lines are both parallel to the same line, then they are parallel to each other.

5. Constant Distance Between Parallel Lines

If two lines are parallel, the perpendicular distance between them is always the same, no matter where you measure it.
